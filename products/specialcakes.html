Special Cakes Website Project

A client-focused frontend development project.


The Special Cakes project was developed during my NSW TAFE IT Certificate III studies, delivering a comprehensive digital solution encompassing both website development and social media management. Starting with thorough client requirements analysis, I created a detailed development plan and built a presentation website featuring all requested functionalities.

Upon completing the website, I expanded the project by assisting the client in establishing their social media presence across multiple platforms. Utilizing social media management tools, I supported content creation, scheduling, and community engagement strategies to effectively grow their online audience.

This project provided valuable experience in end-to-end digital service delivery—from coding and design to digital marketing. Continuous client communication enhanced my understanding of small business needs and challenges during digital transformation.


Features

  • Client-Centric Design: Developed based on client requirements and feedback
  • Interactive Navigation: Hover and active effects for menu items
  • Visual Consistency: Uniform styling and image treatment
  • Product Showcase: Effective presentation of cake products
  • Responsive Layout: Basic mobile-friendly design
  • Contact Information: Clear business details for customers

Tools Used

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3
  • Image Editing: Pixlr E
  • Design: Color theory, typography principles
  • Development Tools: Basic text editor
  • Project Management: Development timeline planning
  • Client Communication: Requirement gathering and feedback implementation
  • Social Media Management Platform: Buffer

My Story

First Half of My Life

I started out as a teacher—a role that taught me more than just how to instruct. It gave me patience, perseverance, and a deep respect for building trust in professional relationships. The challenges I faced in that chapter of life helped shape my mindset and work ethic.

While still working as a teacher, I began exploring graphic design and IT support on the side. What started as a curiosity slowly grew into a real passion. This blend of creativity and technology opened new doors for me and ultimately guided my transition into the IT field.

Most recently, I completed a series of IT courses at TAFE, finishing my Diploma in IT and now preparing to receive my certification. While I may not yet have extensive local experience in Australia, I’m confident that my drive to grow, adapt, and learn will keep pushing me forward in the IT field.

Second Half of My Life

With a foundation built on education, creativity, and a growing technical skillset, I’m now fully focused on building my future in the IT industry.

My experience as a teacher has equipped me with strong communication, problem-solving, and collaboration skills—qualities that are just as valuable in tech. Along the way, I’ve developed a deep interest in technology and digital solutions, and have worked hard to back that passion with real skills through formal IT training and hands-on practice.

I may not have years of local industry experience yet, but I make up for it with motivation, adaptability, and a genuine drive to grow. I’m eager to contribute, to learn from others, and to be part of something meaningful.

I believe that every team deserves someone who is not only technically capable but also open-minded, dependable, and willing to keep growing. I hope to bring that energy wherever I go—and I’d be truly grateful for the opportunity to prove it.
